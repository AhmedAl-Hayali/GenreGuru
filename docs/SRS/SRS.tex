\section{User Documentation and Training}

\subsection{User Documentation Requirements}

To ensure that users can effectively interact with the music generation and recommendation platform, the following user documentation will be provided:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{User Manual}: A comprehensive guide explaining the functionalities of the platform, including step-by-step instructions on generating music, adjusting parameters, and using the recommendation features.
    \item \textbf{Quick Start Guide}: A concise guide aimed at helping new users get started with the basics of generating and recommending music.
    \item \textbf{FAQs and Troubleshooting}: A list of frequently asked questions and troubleshooting tips to help users solve common issues independently.
    \item \textbf{Video Tutorials}: Step-by-step video guides that visually demonstrate key features and workflows.
\end{itemize}

These documents will be designed for users of varying technical backgrounds to ensure they can fully utilize the platform's capabilities.

\subsection{Training Requirements}

To provide users with sufficient knowledge to operate the platform effectively, the following training resources will be developed:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Interactive Training Sessions}: These sessions, either virtual or in-person, will help users—especially music producers and educators—get hands-on experience with the platform’s features.
    \item \textbf{Webinars}: Webinars will be organized to introduce new features, answer user questions, and provide additional insights into advanced usage. They will be recorded for repeat usage.
    \item \textbf{Training Workshops}: Workshops will be conducted for stakeholders to offer deeper knowledge, with a focus on leveraging the platform for specific needs, such as music production or sound design.
\end{itemize}

These training requirements aim to encourage users to explore the full potential of the platform, regardless of their prior experience in music production or technology.
